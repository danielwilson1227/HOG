What We Do

Our mission is to bring hope to the hopeless, food to the hungry, clothes to the needy and show Jesus Christ through our lives. We are a ministry that reaches out to the lost and broken. Our ministry hosts monthly events in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. We provide clothing and groceries to the communities in need along with bringing hopeful messages through the Word of God. When necessary, we may sponsor eligible candidates who cannot afford to go to a life changing program like Teen Challenge. We are also planning on building the Grace Hope Center that will provide a stable place to accomplish these goals.
